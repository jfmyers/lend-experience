Lend Experience's mission is for everyone to discover a career they'll love.

There are too many individuals employed in jobs they aren't deeply passionate about. A lack of easily accessible and organized career knowledge results in recent graduates entering careers that aren't necessarily right for them.

Lend Experience was launched in the spring of 2012 as an answer to this problem. Through a system of localized community teams, Lend Experience connects like-minded students and professionals via live talks and career related opportunities, such as job-shadowing and internships. Community teams also recruit professionals to become contributors of career experiences on the Lend Experience website, which serves as a career knowledge base and bridge between all communities.

Lend Experience is the creation of Jim Myers, Fred Reimer and Steven Dell'Amore, our first community team member.
